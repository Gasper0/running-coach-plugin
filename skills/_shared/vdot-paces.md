## Conversion Formulas (if exact time not in table)

**10km → Semi-marathon equivalent:**
- Semi pace per km ≈ 10km pace + 0:20 to 0:25 min/km

**Semi-marathon → Marathon equivalent:**
- Marathon pace per km ≈ semi pace + 0:15 to 0:25 min/km
- Rough multiplier: marathon time ≈ semi time × 2.1 to 2.15

**Easy pace rule of thumb:**
- Easy (Z1) = goal race pace + 1:30 to 2:00 min/km minimum
- When in doubt, go slower on easy days

**VMA / VO2max pace:**
- Approximately 95-100% of 10km race pace
- For 400m reps: 10km race pace × 0.96
